Who We Are

FoodFoward SA is a non-profit organization dedicated to fighting hunger in South Africa by redistributing surplus food from the supply chain to those in need. Our mission is to reduce food waste and ensure that vulnerable communities have access to nutritious meals.

Our Vision

We envision a South Africa where no one goes hungry and surplus food is used to nourish those who need it most.

Our Work

  • Collecting surplus food from retailers, manufacturers, and farmers
  • Distributing food to registered beneficiary organizations
  • Raising awareness about food insecurity and waste
